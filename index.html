This comprehensive 21-week training program features progressive volume increases with strategic cutback weeks for recovery and adaptation. The plan builds from 30-minute long runs to a peak of 120 minutes, incorporating strength training, mobility work, and race-specific preparation. Focus on consistent effort, proper form, and listening to your body throughout the program.
